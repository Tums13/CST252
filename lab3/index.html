Car Game: War

The selected game is the card game: War. Task 1 describes pseudocoding the card game War. The questions our psedocode will answer are:
1. What actions are take in War?
•Playing and battling a card, taking the losers card, and repeating.
2. What do I need to remember or keep track of?
•Remembering the rules of the game.
3. What I do over and over again?
•Playing a card from your pile.
4. When do I need to make decisions?
•Deciding a victor for each battle.

•Players shuffle deck and split it
•Each player flips one card over from their pile of cards
•The players compare their card value to one another
•If player 1 has a higher valued card then player 2, player 1 wins that “battle”. Vice Versa.
•If the players tie, the players flip another card over and compare those. The victor of that “battle” wins all pairs of tied battles.
•The players continue this loop until each player's deck pile runs out.
•The player with the most battles won (more cards) wins the game.
